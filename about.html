About Me

I’m Nikhil, a creator focused on understanding human behavior, attention, and self-improvement. I write to help people think better and live more intentionally.

My Journey

I started my journey in 2019, creating content on YouTube and Twitter about psychology, attention, and self-improvement. Over time, I’ve built a community of people who are passionate about learning and growing together.

My Approach

I believe that the key to creating impactful content lies in understanding how the human mind works. By applying principles from psychology and neuroscience, I help creators build ideas that are not only engaging but also meaningful.
